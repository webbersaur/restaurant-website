Our Story

At Sly Fox Tavern, we believe in great food, cold drinks, and good company. Our kitchen serves up classic American fare with a twist, using fresh local ingredients to create dishes that keep you coming back.

Cozy up by the fireplace, catch the game on one of our TVs, or gather with friends for a night out. We're your neighborhood spot for good times.
