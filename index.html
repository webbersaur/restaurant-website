Our Story

At Ember & Oak, we believe in honoring the roots of American cuisine while pushing its boundaries. Our kitchen sources the finest local ingredients, transforming them into dishes that surprise and delight.

Every plate tells a story of craftsmanship, creativity, and a deep respect for the land that feeds us.
